What is Plate of Nations?

Crafted by the MLK Business Association, Plate of Nations is an electrifying event that promotes the eclectic mix of independently-owned restaurants nestled in the vibrant MLK community. This two-week long annual festival is a celebration of culture and diversity as it showcases the mouthwatering delights of immigrant-owned businesses in the Rainier Valley.

Project Overview

Our team embarked on a mission to elevate the online presence of Plate of Nations by breathing new life into its website design, centering around:

  • Authentic cultural immersion and representation
  • Accessibility to non-native English speakers
  • Mobile-first design for optimal user experience
